## Overview

Welcome to **PrepGen: Your AI Career Coach for Professional Success** — a smart, AI-powered career assistant designed to help students, freshers, and professionals confidently navigate their job-hunting journey. From building professional resumes to preparing for interviews, PrepGen empowers you with tailored AI tools and up-to-date industry insights — all in one platform.

Whether you're crafting your first resume, need a cover letter that stands out, or want to sharpen your interview skills through interactive quizzes, **PrepGen** has your back.

---

## Key Features

- 🎨 **AI-Powered Resume Builder**: Instantly generate a polished, ATS-friendly resume tailored to your role and experience level.
- ✉️ **AI Cover Letter Generator**: Create professional, customized cover letters based on your target job and resume.
- 📝 **Interview Preparation Quizzes**: Practice job interview questions and track your performance through engaging, skill-specific quizzes.
- 📊 **Performance Graphs**: Visualize your quiz results, track progress over time, and identify areas to improve.
- 📈 **Industry Insights Dashboard**: Stay updated on the latest tech trends — discover which programming languages, frameworks, and roles are in demand, along with salary insights.
- 🌐 **Modern, Accessible UI**: A clean, responsive interface designed for ease of use on both desktop and mobile.

---

## How it Works

1. **Sign Up or Log In**: Get started with a secure account.
2. **Build Your Career Toolkit**: Use AI tools to generate resumes and cover letters tailored to your goals.
3. **Practice and Learn**: Take quizzes designed for specific job roles, tech stacks, and interview scenarios.
4. **Track and Improve**: View detailed performance graphs and progress reports.
5. **Explore Industry Trends**: Gain valuable insights about tech trends, job salaries, and popular career paths.

---

## Technologies Behind PrepGen

- **Next.js**: Core framework for full-stack web development.
- **shadcn/ui**: Modern, reusable UI component library for clean and consistent design.
- **Tailwind CSS**: Utility-first CSS framework for rapid UI styling.
- **Prisma & NeonDB**: Modern, serverless relational database with powerful ORM.
- **Clerk Authentication**: Secure, easy-to-use auth for user accounts.
- **Zod**: Robust schema validation for data integrity.
- **Inngest**: Serverless job queues and scheduling.
- **Gemini API (Google AI)**: AI model powering content generation tools.
- **React Hook Form & React Query**: For seamless form handling and data fetching.
- **Recharts**: Interactive and responsive data visualization for performance tracking.
- **Sentry**: Error monitoring and performance analytics.
